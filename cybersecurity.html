Cybersecurity

Cyber attacks malicious attempts to access or damage a computer or network system. Cyberattacks can lead to the loss of money or the theft of personal, financial and medical information. These attacks can damage your reputation and safety. Many of today's cyber criminals use a variety of tactics to exploit there targets, one well known trick is Social Engineering. Social Engineering the attacker tricks you into revealing sensitive information

This is where a Cybersecurity Professional "Pentester" comes in to play. Pentesters are trained to be ethical hackers and test companies networks for vulnerabilities so they can patch any area of their network that may be vulnerable to an attack. Their has been a projected 33% increase for the need of cybersecurity professionals from 2020 to 2030, the industry is moving rapidly and more and more companies are being hacked on a daily basis. Average annual salary for Ethical Hackers is $103,590 in 2020. Hack legally and get paid what more could you ask for.
