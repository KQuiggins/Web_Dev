In summary of the pages in this website we have discussed many different technologies, programming languages and careers and don't get me wrong I Love technology and programming, but there is always a down side to everything so let's discuss just a couple.

Imposter Syndrome: with impostor syndrome you doubt your skills , talents, or accomplishments and have an internalized fear of being exposed as a fraud. Despite all the external evidence of their competence, people who experience this feel as though they don't deserve their success of luck. This is quite common in the programming and cyber security world because there is always someone who knows more than you and the amount of competition can be really overwhelming.

Skill Gap: In the IT and programming world there is a vast amount of information to learn and a lot of people give up because of impostor syndrome and thinking they will never be able to learn everything. The goal isn't to learn everything because that isn't possible you will always keep learning in this field you never stop and someone will always know more than you when you realize this things get easier.
